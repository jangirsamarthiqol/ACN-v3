# Prompt 3: ACN Home - Banner Carousel Section

Design an engaging banner carousel section for real estate app:

## CAROUSEL CONTAINER
- Full width minus 16px side margins
- Height: 180px
- Border radius: 16px
- Auto-play with 4-second intervals (static image showing one banner)
- Swipeable indicator (slight peek of next slide)

## BANNER CARDS (Show first banner prominently)
- Gradient backgrounds (use vibrant real estate themed colors)
- Option 1 (SHOWN): Blue to purple gradient (#0066CC to #8B5CF6)
- Option 2: Green to teal gradient (#00C853 to #00BCD4)
- Option 3: Orange to red gradient (#FF9800 to #F44336)

## BANNER CONTENT LAYOUT
- Left side (60%):
  - Headline text (white, bold, 20px): "Premium Properties"
  - Subtext (white, 14px, opacity 0.9): "Exclusive deals this week"
  - CTA button: "Explore Now" (white background, primary text #0066CC, 8px radius, 12px padding)
- Right side (40%): Illustrative icon or minimal inventory graphic (white/light outline style)
- Padding: 20px all around
- Content vertically centered

## INDICATORS
- Dot indicators at bottom center of banner
- Active dot: White color, 8px width, slightly elongated (12px)
- Inactive dots: White with 50% opacity, 6px circular
- 4px spacing between dots
- 3 dots total
- Position: 12px from bottom

## VISUAL STYLE
- Premium glass-like feel
- Subtle inner shadow for depth
- Clean, minimal iconography
- Professional but engaging appearance

- Appears below the search section

- Part of scrollable home content
